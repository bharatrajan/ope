Our Product

We propose to replace the Conventional controller by a novel artificial intelligent neural network (ANN) controller. After the offline training, the ANN will be able to provide optimal and truly decoupled control for a solar inverter in real time.

The proposed ANN control technology has been tested successfully under many challenging dynamic and complex simulation environments and hard experiment conditions. Compared to conventional control methods, the ANN control produces the fastest response time, lowest overshoot, and, in general, the closest to ideal performance. In addition, the ANN controller can automatically determine a best and reliable approach to control the power converter when there is a trend for the inverter to operate beyond its physical constraints, and can connect a solar inverter to the grid in almost zero time without the need for pre-synchronization while oscillation and overshoot of the system is almost unnoticeable.
