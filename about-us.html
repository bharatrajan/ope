About us

SMART signifies, ability to determine what is best, and adapt and react to it. Orange Power Electric defines the meaning of Smart by providing intelligent electrical solutions for distributed generated sources. US Electricity Grid is undergoing significant transformation for integrating renewable and distributed generated sources and bidirectional flow of electricity to and from the Consumers, this introduces several challenges like Power Quality, reliability and Security. Our first product is a smart Inverter for integrating renewable energy sources to the grid, allowing a bidirectional power flow and providing a stable and reliable operating condition.

Our main goal is to bring down the cost of Inverters to less than $0.20 / watt along with providing a reliable grid integration inverter system for distributed generated sources. By minimizing power losses, Volt-VAR, and CVR with increased energy efficiency we will commercialize our product to support higher penetration of distributed generated sources (starting with PV) and optimizing performance from grid interaction along with testing on different utility business models and advanced grid functionality at different sites and conditions.
